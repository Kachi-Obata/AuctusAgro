Products & Specifications

Bulk cashew kernels for importers, wholesalers, ingredient buyers, and brands across Europe and the Middle East.

Primary Grades

We offer a range of grades to meet different application and pricing needs:

W320

Our core grade, featuring whole white kernels with consistent size and quality. Suitable for premium retail, foodservice, and processing applications.

Splits

High-quality splits available for value-driven applications where appearance is less critical but quality remains important.

Other Grades

Additional grades such as W240, W450, and kernel pieces are available upon request to match specific product requirements.
