Export & Logistics

Structured for repeat B2B trade into Europe and the Middle East, predictable documentation, clear milestones, and buyer-ready handoffs.

FOB Lagos

Our most commonly recommended option for importers, providing buyers with maximum control over freight, insurance, and destination handling.

CFR / CIF

Available upon request for major destination ports. These options can simplify logistics by allowing us to arrange freight (and insurance under CIF) as part of the shipment.

EXW

Available for specific buyer setups where the buyer manages full logistics from our facility.

The final Incoterm is agreed based on the destination, buyer preference, and overall shipping plan to ensure clarity, cost efficiency, and smooth delivery.
